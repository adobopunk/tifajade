My friends call me TJ.

If you're reading us, consider us friends! I’m an animator passionate about telling stories through motion, blending artistic vision with purposeful design to create meaningful experiences. Previously, I was a video editor at UFC. Currently, I'm a freelance motion designer and the Animation Director at BoomTV. I have a multidisciplinary background that blends my experience with video production, motion design, programming, and 3D animation. My work is driven by a passion to help improve everyday lives, always striving to connect with and inspire the communities I engage with.

I studied film, which allowed me to dive into every aspect of the process—art direction, cinematography, sound design, music composition, and more. Eventually, I realized: what better way to bring all these elements together than through a career in animation? In my free time you can developing film, updating this website, or looking for the best tacos in Seattle. Thanks for checking out my portfolio, and I hope you've enjoyed your time here!
