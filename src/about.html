My friends call me TJ.

If you're reading this, consider us friends! I have a talent for telling stories through motion. I built this website from the ground up and I continue to work on improving it. Don't hesitate to reach out if you want to talk motion design, or just want to know where my favorite Thai food here in Seattle is. Thanks for stopping by!
