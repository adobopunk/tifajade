My friends call me TJ.

And if you're reading us, consider us friends! I’m a freelance 3D motion designer and Animation Director at BoomTV, blending my love for motion design, visual effects, and storytelling into every project. My journey has been far from traditional—I'm a self-taught VFX artist who spent spent four years as a video editor for UFC. These days I spend a lot of time doing R&D, exploring the intersection of motion design and 3D art.

I'm always looking for ways to improve my capabilities. In fact, I learned how to code just so I could make this portfolio, and I'm constantly developing it. Hopefully you'll revisit it from time to time and see what new features I've added! I’m always working on new ways to push my design skills forward. I have a background in music production and love crafting audioreactive visuals.

My passion for animation and motion design comes from the way different disciplines coalesce into something whole. I studied film in college, and my background as a filmmaker helped me realize I was interested in every aspect of the process - art direction, cinematography, sound design, music composition, etc. Eventually I realized: what better way to make use of all these elements, than through a career in animation? Thanks for checking out my portfolio, and I hope you enjoyed your time here!
