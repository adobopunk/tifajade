My friends call me TJ.

If you're reading this, consider us friends! I provide a variety of creative services catered for animation, including direction, motion graphics, VFX, 3D modeling, and concept art. My background is multidisciplinary - as a film student, I fell in love with every aspect of the creative process. I carried that passion for juggling different disciplines into a career in motion design, and I've never stopped learning.

I designed motion graphics at my hometown NBC affiliate, and eventually joined the UFC as a video editor with my work featured on ESPN and UFC Fight Pass. Today, I'm a freelance motion designer and the Animation Director at BoomTV. I help brands conceptualize videos, design motion systems, and build looping animations for screens of all shapes and sizes. Shoot me an email if you'd like to say hi, or visit the contact form at the bottom of this website to start a more formal project proposal. Thanks for stopping by!
